I would like to contribute to the smart operation of renewable-based distributed resources and load demand in smart grids using machine learning and mathematical programming with cutting-edge technologies and tools

I am Data Scientist, especialized in optimization and predictive modelling, pursuing a PhD in Electrical, Electronics and Automation Engineering in Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. The thesis is dedicated to the prediction of power losses at large-scale smart grids under uncertianty using machine learning models as well as the optimization using mathematical programming of smart grids with presence of distributed resources such as photovoltaics, Electric Vehicles and energy storage.
