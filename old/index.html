About me

I am a 4th-year PhD. student at the IMDEA Software Institute under the supervision of Pierre Ganty, expecting to graduate before October 2020.

Before joining IMDEA, I studied a Double Degree in Mathematics and Computer Science at Universidad Autónoma de Madrid.

You can find further information about me in my CV.

Some of my research interests

  • Weighted automata and weighted context-free grammars and their applications to software verification and ML
  • Parikh abstraction and other regular approximations of context-free languages
  • Finite-state automata and optimization algorithms: minimization, residualization
  • Learning automata
